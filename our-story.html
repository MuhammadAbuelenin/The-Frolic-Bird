Our STORY

The Frolic Bird Journey started in October 2010 as a buying office, agent for global importers, and stock trader in Cairo, Egypt. The company was operating under the name “Link trading” up until October.2017. during those 7 years, Ezzat learnt a lot in link as an old school. Link trading happened to work with giant international fashion brands only in the USA market.

In 2016, after 5 years from the Egyptian revolution, the Egyptian government floated the Egyptian pound against the US Dollar, which has made a dramatic change in our profits. So, we decided to set the company on-hold until further notice.

In 2017, Ezzat decided to reactivate the business and change the business strategy to work with the European markets. So, In 2018, Ezzat travelled to Sweden to do masters in International Marketing from Jönköping university in Sweden.

In 2019, Ezzat received a master degree in business administration majoring in International marketing from Jönköping university in Sweden. The masters research study was about the Swedish consumer behaviour towards the functional food and drinks. This makes a big part of the frolic bird is meant to market internationally the sustainable products especially from Ezzat’s home country Egypt.

From 2020- 2021, it was the come back again. Ezzat rebranded the company to become “The Frolic Bird” and decided to move our head office to Jönköping, Sweden for International Marketing while keeping both the operations office in Egypt and the same sister company that handles our logistics.

We also updated the business model of the company and spread awareness among more than 100 factories in the garment and textile industry in Egypt to stay tuned for deals that we will do on behalf of our esteemed European clients. Now, we have hands-on experience in both the USA and European markets.
