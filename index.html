what we do

The Frolic Bird is an international marketing hub based in jönköping sweden. From one side, we help fashion brands worldwide to outsource their manufacturing activity in Egypt. From the other side, we do international marketing for the Egyptian manufacturers across the globe.

Vission, Mission & Target

Vision

The most trusted international marketing hub in Europe. We aspire to be the number one stop destination for international fashion brands who strive to outsource their manufacturing facilities across borders especially in Egypt.

  • Learn More

Mission

Is to outsource the manufacturing process of the European international fashion brands in Egypt. Also, to do international marketing for the whole textile and garment industry in Egypt and present the added value in the Scandinavian markets.

  • Learn More

Target

Our target is the Scandinavian market as a start. We also in Egypt target only the manufacturers who have sustainable and unique materials that can find places on the global map of textile and
garment.
