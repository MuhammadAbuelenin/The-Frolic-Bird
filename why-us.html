Why US

Because of the golden triangle: Perfect Quality, Affordable Price, and Accurate Delivery.

Our team is expert and specialized in the ideal standards of garments quality worldwide for more than 25 years in the global market.

Our experiences allowed us to understand the client’s requirements in the initial conversation so fast and deliver the goods in the right time.

We are experts in all means of shipping and logistics in details for more than 20 years to export to any destination around the world according to the client’s request.

Our experience in sourcing the materials allowed us to always offer a competitive price to our clients.

We have access to more than 100 garment factories all over Egypt.

We are flexible enough in the communication with our clients whenever there is a problem that threats our client’s business. Our client’s business is our business.
