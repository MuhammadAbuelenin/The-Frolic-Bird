Vision, Mission & Target

Vision

The most trusted international marketing hub in Europe. We aspire to be the number one stop destination for international fashion brands who strive to outsource their manufacturing facilities across borders especially in Egypt.

Mission

Is to outsource the manufacturing process of the European international fashion brands in Egypt. Also, to do international marketing for the whole textile and garment industry in Egypt and present the added value in the Scandinavian markets.

Target

Our target is the Scandinavian market as a start. We also in Egypt target only the manufacturers who have sustainable and unique materials that can find places on the global map of textile and garment.
